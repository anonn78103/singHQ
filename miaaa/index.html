Char Kadam

English Translation of the Song

With your hand in mine,
Just four steps, only four steps,
Come walk a little with me…

Without words spoken, without words heard,
With your hand in mine,
Just four steps, only four steps,
Come walk a little with me…

If sunlight troubles you on the path,
I’ll spread shade around you.
If the darkness frightens you,
I’ll place the moon in the sky for you.

When sadness surrounds you,
I’ll tell you jokes to make you smile.
Smiling, laughing, softly humming,
Together we’ll walk those four steps…

If I find a path where you walk beside me,
Then why fear the world at all?
Not just four steps, but a whole lifetime,
I’ll gladly walk with you.

Without words spoken, without words heard,
With your hand in mine,
Just four steps, only four steps,
Come walk a little with me…

From the movie PK (2014)
